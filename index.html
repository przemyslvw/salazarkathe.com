The Artist's Manifesto

My work focuses on the exploration, investigation, creation, and representation of ritualistic, aesthetic, and mystical concepts native to the Caribbean region. The objective is to detach these practices from the taboos that often surround them, highlighting instead their historical value and their relevance in the social construction of our collective identity.

Exploring Caribbean Mysticism

Katerine Salazar Villamil's artistic journey is rooted in a profound exploration of the Caribbean's rich spiritual heritage. Her work serves as a bridge between ancestral wisdom and contemporary artistic expression, creating a dialogue that transcends time and cultural boundaries.

Through her innovative approach, she challenges conventional perceptions of ritualistic practices, transforming what has often been misunderstood or stigmatized into powerful visual narratives that celebrate the depth and complexity of Caribbean mysticism.

Each piece in her collection represents a careful investigation into the aesthetic and spiritual dimensions of her cultural heritage, offering viewers an opportunity to engage with these traditions through a lens of respect, understanding, and artistic beauty.
